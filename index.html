I am Zhenyu Liu, a 1st year PhD student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), working with Dr. Liu Liu. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2023.

My research focus is to democratize cutting-edge AI technology on everyday devices via developing efficient and robust AI algorithms and co-designing the corresponding hardware accelerators towards a triple-win in accuracy, efficiency, and robustness.
